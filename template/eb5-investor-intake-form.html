EB-5 Investor Visa Program Assessment The EB-5 program provides permanent residence to investors who invest $800,000-$1,050,000 in a U.S. commercial enterprise that creates or preserves at least 10 full-time jobs for U.S. workers. This is the most direct investment-based pathway to U.S. citizenship for high-net-worth families.

Total Investment: $800K-$1.05M capital + $50K-$75K+ in legal/administrative fees | Timeline: 24-48 months
