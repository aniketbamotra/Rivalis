Matter of Dhanasar Three-Prong Test for National Interest Waiver

Your petition must demonstrate:

  1. Substantial Merit & National Importance: The proposed endeavor has both substantial merit and national importance
  2. Well Positioned to Advance: You are well positioned to advance the proposed endeavor
  3. Beneficial to Waive Requirements: On balance, it would be beneficial to the United States to waive the job offer and labor certification requirements
This is the foundation of your NIW case - be clear and specific
