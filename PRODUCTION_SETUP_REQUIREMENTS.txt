1. SUPABASE (Database & Authentication)
================================================================================

What it does: Manages your user accounts, stores form submissions, and handles 
all your website's data securely.

Steps to get your keys:
1. Go to https://supabase.com and sign in (or create a free account)
2. Click "New Project" and create a project (choose a region close to your users)
3. Wait for the project to finish setting up (takes 2-3 minutes)
4. Once ready, go to Project Settings (gear icon on left sidebar)

First, get your Project URL:
5. Click on "Data API" in the settings menu

□ Project URL
  Example: https://yourproject.supabase.co
  Found under: "Data API" section → "Project URL"
  Copy the full URL shown

Then, get your API keys:
6. Go back to settings and click on "API Keys"
7. At the top, click on the "Legacy anon, service_role API keys" tab

□ Anon/Public Key
  Example: eyJhbGc... (long string of characters)
  Found under: "Legacy anon, service_role API keys" tab → Look for "anon public"
  Click the eye icon to reveal, then copy button to copy

□ Service Role Key (Keep this SECRET!)
  Example: eyJhbGc... (different long string)
  Found under: "Legacy anon, service_role API keys" tab → Look for "service_role"
  Click the eye icon to reveal, then copy button to copy
  ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Never share this publicly or commit to code

Note: We'll handle the database setup and configuration for you.


================================================================================
2. STRIPE (Payment Processing)
================================================================================

What it does: Processes credit card payments for consultations and services 
securely. Handles the $499 emergency consultation fee and other service payments.

Steps to get your keys:
1. Go to https://stripe.com and sign in to your account
   (If you don't have one, create a business account at https://dashboard.stripe.com/register)
2. Complete your business profile in Stripe (required to accept real payments)
3. In the Stripe Dashboard, click "Developers" in the top menu
4. Click "API keys" in the left sidebar

You'll need to provide us with these 3 items:

□ Publishable Key (Live Mode)
  Example: pk_live_51SeD...
  Found under: "Standard keys" → "Publishable key"
  ⚠️ Make sure you're in "Live mode" (toggle at top right should say "Live")

□ Secret Key (Live Mode - Keep this SECRET!)
  Example: sk_live_51SeD...
  Found under: "Standard keys" → "Secret key"
  Click "Reveal live key token" to see it
  ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Keep this completely private

To get your Webhook Secret:
1. In Stripe Dashboard, go to "Developers" → "Webhooks"
2. Click "+ Add endpoint"
3. For "Endpoint URL", enter: https://rivalislaw.com/.netlify/functions/stripe-webhook
4. Click "Select events" and choose these events:
   - checkout.session.completed
   - payment_intent.succeeded
5. Click "Add endpoint"
6. After creating, click on the webhook you just made
7. Click "Reveal" next to "Signing secret"

□ Webhook Secret
  Example: whsec_...
  Found after: Creating the webhook and revealing the signing secret
  Note: We'll guide you through this step after initial deployment

3. RESEND (Email Service)
================================================================================

What it does: Sends emails to users and admins when forms are submitted, 
emergency consultations are requested, etc.

Steps to get your key:
1. Go to https://resend.com and sign up for an account
2. Verify your email address
3. After logging in, go to "API Keys" in the left sidebar
4. Click "Create API Key"
5. Give it a name like "Rivalis Production"
6. Select "Full access" permission
7. Click "Add"
8. Copy the key immediately (you won't be able to see it again!)

□ Resend API Key
  Example: re_...
  Found under: API Keys section after creation

To set up your domain for sending emails:
1. In Resend, go to "Domains" in the left sidebar
2. Click "Add Domain"
3. Enter: rivalislaw.com
4. Follow the DNS verification steps shown (we can help with this)
5. Wait for verification (usually 15-30 minutes)

□ Admin Email for Notifications
  Your email for receiving emergency consultation alerts: _______________________
  Example: admin@rivalislaw.com or your-email@yourdomain.com

□ From Email for Sending
  Email address to send notifications from: _______________________
  Example: noreply@rivalislaw.com or notifications@rivalislaw.com
  Note: Must be from your verified domain in Resend

Note: Until domain is verified, we'll use Resend's test domain for testing.
